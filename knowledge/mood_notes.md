# Mood Notes

## happy
Happy profiles usually fit songs with bright valence, optimistic lift, and movement that feels forward instead of heavy.

## chill
Chill profiles tend to prefer lower-pressure pacing, softer edges, and a relaxed emotional temperature.

## intense
Intense profiles usually reward pressure, urgency, and tracks that keep emotional tension active.

## relaxed
Relaxed profiles often fit music with gentle momentum and low-friction transitions.

## focused
Focused profiles usually benefit from steady energy, repeatable structure, and minimal emotional clutter.

## moody
Moody profiles often connect with darker atmosphere, restraint, and emotional ambiguity.

## euphoric
Euphoric profiles generally reward uplift, release, and emotionally expansive energy.

## tender
Tender profiles often fit softness, intimacy, and emotionally warm detail.

## sensual
Sensual profiles often respond to groove, warmth, and smooth pacing more than speed.

## chaotic
Chaotic profiles usually reward sharp contrast, rapid changes, and high-intensity detail.

## reflective
Reflective profiles often fit quieter movement, gentler pacing, and room to think.
